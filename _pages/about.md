I am a first-year PhD candidate in the College of Engineering at [City University of Hong Kong](https://www.cityu.edu.hk/). My research focuses on tropical cyclone (TC) dynamics, with particular interests in intensification, eyewall replacement cycles (ERC), and climatological of TCs. I was fortunate to receive my master’s training under Professor [Qiaoyan Wu](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=xtOljf4AAAAJ&hl=en), and I am currently supervised by Professor [Ping Lu](https://scholars.cityu.edu.hk/en/persons/plu222/). I am excited to further explore the physical processes governing TC behavior and contribute to advancing our understanding of these extreme weather events.
